About Us

The Dream of a man since he was at his teen age led the foundation for one of the best food product company that will spread smile all over the globe. The man who led the foundation is Dr.Nayim B.Maner and his dream came to life in the form of MANCO food products pvt.ltd. With an aim to manufacture the food products in India and lower the burden of importing food products from foreign countries MANCO has launched 80 different types of snacks. All the products are made with considering diverse taste of Indian people so that all the family together can enjoy on brand. The company is now run by the two sons’ of Dr. Nayim namely Mr.Aasim Maner and Mr.Zishan Ali Maner. They are reputed personalities in Miraj City for their social service. MANCO will one day be the leading brand in Indian as well International market.
